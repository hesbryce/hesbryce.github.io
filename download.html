How to Find Your Share Code

1

Open the App on Your Apple Watch

Launch either IRL Health Bar or Workout Health Bar on your Apple Watch.

2

Navigate to Settings

Scroll down and tap the Settings button at the bottom of the app.

3

Find Web Sharing

Look for the "Web Sharing" or "Share Code" section in the settings menu.

4

Generate or View Your Code

Your 8-character share code will be displayed (e.g., AB12@#34). Tap to generate a new code if needed.

5

Enter on the Website

Go to the home page and enter your share code to connect your health bar to the web.

Privacy First

Share codes expire after 24 hours and can be regenerated anytime. Your health data stays secure and private - only people with your active share code can view your health bar.
