IRL Health Bar

2021, I used my Apple Watch health data and Python to extract, make visualizations, and use ML Algorithms Linear & Log Regression, and K-Clustering, to predict an impromptu Ultra Marathon Finish. After my accomplishments I wanted to educate others to run better and recover better using data.

2022 White board of the final vision after several iterations. And inital failures when Apple introduce Heart Rate Zones. Listening to feedback the implementation needed to be more simplified and summarized, as well as have more real world implications than what HR Zones provided.

2024 Attended WWDC 24 in person showcasing my WorkoutKit, HealthKit, and SwiftUI app. I used feedback to drive 100%+ app growth, then attended following-up events in person at Apple Park Cupertino (International Women's Day, visionOS Design and Game Development, WWDC 25 virtual office hours)

2025 scaled app to over 6,000 downloads, with major updates in design, development, and scalibility of IRL Health Bar beyond Apple Watch
